In this lab, students will use the natural luminescence of genetically modified E. coli bacteria to create unique and eye-catching artworks. The E. coli The stars of the show are the E. coli bacteria. These organisms have been engineered to express Green Fluorescent Protein (GFP), a protein that gives off a bright green glow when exposed to ultraviolet light. This means that the E. coli colonies will literally shine, providing a living canvas for artistic expression. The Petri Dishes The Petri dishes are the artists' palettes. Once the E. coli has grown into colonies, the Petri dishes are transformed into glowing artworks. Students can use different colors of agar, stencils, or even specialized media to add contrast and dimension to their creations. The final pieces can be displayed under UV light, creating a mesmerizing and otherworldly experience. Conclusion The Fluorescent Art Lab is a unique and exciting space that allows students to unleash their creativity while learning about science. It is a place where art and science merge, creating an unforgettable experience.
